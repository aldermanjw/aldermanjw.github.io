New Era, PC gaming

The start of PC gaming rose with the "Video Game crash of 1983" for years PC games florished the market becoming popular. The early success was based on the Technological advancement of computers. Computers were no longer something that took up entire gymnasiums, they have evolved in a household appliance. But, during the Late-1990s another mile stone for the gaming industry had occured and PC started losing its traction.

Companys like Microsoft, Nintendo, and Sony all were putting out consoles which were devices specially used for playing video games. Blooming what we call the "Console War." However, like a pheonix from the ashes the PC rose to the insane power house it is today.

The turn of a century, began the Era of PC gaming, computers have become powerful they were no longer slow 16bit processors techology had evoloved. In decade PC gaming have single handely changed the thought process of gamers.

How to be a Happy PC gamer

Every gamer is differnt. With so many differnt types and titles out there here are some tips to pick the game thats right for you.

  • Know what your computer can handle and what it can't. Some PCs have no problem with high-end graphics while others will have trouble keeping up with a game


  • Find what games you like. Action, Adventure, Online, or Sandbox. Find what you enjoy


  • Steam is great but, also a money blackhole. Remember just because you bought a game for $5 during the Summer Sale does not mean you're going to play it.


  • Are you Competitive or Casual? If you don't like the stress then just find games you can play by yourself. If you love competition then go for games that brings it out in you


  • Have fun, Gaming has come a really long way to the point it is a sport. But, we game because we love it so when you're not having fun whats the point?
